How To Use

Enjoying soft, fluffy, perfectly golden pooris is easy. Follow these simple steps for the best taste.

Step 1: Prepare

Cut open the pack and remove the pooris gently. Keep them ready on a clean plate for frying.

Step 2: Fry

Heat oil in a pan. Once hot, gently slide in the pooris and fry until they puff up and turn golden.

Step 3: Enjoy

Serve hot with potato masala, chole, kurma, or any curry of your choice.
