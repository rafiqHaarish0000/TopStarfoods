How To Use

Preparing soft, fresh idiyappam is simple. Follow these steps for perfect results every time.

Step 1: Prepare

Cut open the pack and place the idiyappam gently on a steaming plate or idli tray.

Step 2: Steam

Steam for 3–5 minutes until soft and warm. No oil required — just pure, fresh goodness.

Step 3: Enjoy

Serve hot with coconut milk, vegetable stew, kurma, or sweetened coconut mixture.
