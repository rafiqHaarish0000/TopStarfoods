How To Use

Enjoying the perfect Soft Poratta is easy. Follow these simple steps for the best, fluffy texture.

Step 1: Prepare

Remove the porattas from the pack and place them on a clean plate. No thawing needed—use directly from the pack.

Step 2: Heat

Heat on a tawa or pan for 30–40 seconds on each side. Press gently to fluff up the layers until soft, warm, and aromatic.

Step 3: Enjoy

Serve hot with salna, curry, or your favorite gravies. Perfect for breakfast, dinner, or snack time!
