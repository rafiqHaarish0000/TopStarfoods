How To Use

Enjoy the authentic homemade taste by following these simple steps.

Step 1: Prepare

Add 2–3 spoons of Idly Powder to a small plate or bowl. Perfect for idly, dosa, paniyaram, or even curd rice!

Step 2: Heat

Add sesame oil (gingelly oil) for the best aroma and taste. Mix gently until it becomes a smooth, flavorful paste.

Step 3: Enjoy

Dip your hot idlies or dosas in the mixture and enjoy the traditional homemade spicy taste instantly!
